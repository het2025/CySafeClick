QR Code Sticker Scam

How it works: Scammers print their own UPI QR codes on stickers and place them directly over the legitimate QR codes at petrol pumps, restaurants, or parking meters.

When you scan it, the money goes to the scammer instead of the shopkeeper.

Prevention Tips:

  • Always check the Payee Name on your phone screen before entering your UPI PIN. Does it match the shop's name?
  • Look closely at the physical stand. Is there a sticker peeling off?
  • Show the confirmation screen to the shopkeeper.

The "Receive Money" Scam

A scammer on OLX/Quikr says they want to buy your item and sends you a QR code, saying "Scan this to receive your payment".

You NEVER need to scan a QR code or enter your UPI PIN to receive money. Scanning a QR code only SENDS money.
