    Password Safety FAQ

    Why length matters more than complexity? +
    Adding just one character increases the possible combinations exponentially. A 12-character simple word is often harder to crack than an 8-character complex one.
    What is a passphrase? +
    A passphrase is a sequence of random words (e.g., "mango-blue-bicycle"). They are easy for humans to remember but very hard for computers to guess.
    India-specific: Is OTP enough? +
    No. OTP is a second layer, but if your password is weak, hackers can still try to trick you into sharing the OTP. Never share your OTP with anyone.
