File Official Complaint Online

Steps to File Complaint

  • Visit Official Portal

    Go to cybercrime.gov.in and click on 'File a Complaint'.

  • Select Category

    Choose between 'Financial Fraud', 'Women/Child Related Crime', or 'Other Cyber Crimes'.

  • Fill Details

    Provide your mobile number, OTP, and details of the incident.

  • Upload Evidence

    Attach screenshots of messages, transaction receipts, and URLs.

  • Submit & Save

    Submit and note down the Acknowledgement Number for future tracking.

Evidence Checklist

Gather these before you start reporting:

  • Screenshots of the scam message/call log
  • Transaction ID / UTR Number (for money theft)
  • Bank statement showing unauthorized debit
  • Phone number of the scammer
  • Website URL or App name used
  • Date and exact time of incident
